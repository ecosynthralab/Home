## About

Ecosynthra Lab is a research group building open geospatial and AI tools for ecological monitoring, invasive species management, and vegetation phenology in Southeast Nigeria and the wider West African forest–savanna ecotone. We work at the intersection of remote sensing, field ecology, and low-cost instrumentation, with a strong commitment to open science and locally-built capacity.

The lab is led by **Dr. Chidimma Maduka**, an Invasion Biogeographer, and operates a TETFund Institutional Based Research programme on invasive alien plant species (IAPS) and vegetation phenology.

## What we work on

- 🌱 **Invasive Alien Plant Species (IAPS)** — species distribution modelling, ethnobotanical drivers of management decisions, and adaptive-cluster field sampling across Anambra and Enugu States
- 🛰️ **Earth Observation pipelines** — Google Earth Engine workflows for phenology, anomaly detection, and land-cover change
- 📡 **Open hardware** — EcoSig-1, a purpose-built handheld multispectral field radiometer for satellite-matched vegetation spectral libraries
- 🌳 **Phenological early warning systems** — theoretical and applied work on ecotonal tipping points and critical slowing down
- 🧰 **Field data tools** — lightweight, open apps for GPS-based field data capture and EO data exploration

## Featured projects

| Project | Description | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **EPIS** | Ecological Phenology Intelligence System — SOS/POS/EOS phenometrics, anomaly detection, stability index, and tipping-point modules built in GEE | Active |
| **EcoSig-1** | Handheld multispectral field radiometer (AS7343 sensor, Sentinel-2/Landsat-matched bands) for building the first open spectral library for SE Nigerian vegetation | Prototype |
| **IAPS SDM Pipeline** | MaxEnt species distribution modelling in GEE for *Chromolaena odorata* and *Mimosa* spp., built on 126 QAQC'd field presence points | Active |
| **PhenoTIP** | Theoretical framework for a phenological tipping-point early warning system for the forest–savanna ecotone | Conceptual |
| **Field Plot Logger** | Web app for GPS-based invasive species plot mapping with photo reference and CSV/JSON export | [Live](https://field-plot-logger.netlify.app) |
| **EO Data Explorer** | Lightweight browser tool for exploring Earth observation datasets | [Live](https://eo-data-explorer.netlify.app) |
| **FLORA-West** | Floristic and Landscape Observation and Remote Sensing Analysis for West Africa — biodiversity intelligence platform | In development |
